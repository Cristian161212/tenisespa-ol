Introduction

This page is about Spanish tennis, here we are going to say all the most important Spanish tennis players, good memories that they have left us and, above all, learn a little more about tennis.

Referentes

Rafa nadal

He is the best of all the tennis players of Spain, has a lot of titles.

Manuel Santana

He stood out for being a true pioneer of the sport in our country (in the 60s), in fact, he was the first who was able to win a Grand Slam , winning a total of in his entire career. Without a doubt, until the arrival of Rafa Nadal, solely because of his great historical contribution, he should be considered one of the best in Spain.

Juan Carlos Ferrero

Winner of 16 ATP titles and finalist of three Grand Slams , winning one of them (the Roland Garros) he even managed to be number one in the ATP ranking in 2003 for 8 weeks .

Carlos Moyá

Finalist of two Grand Slams and winner of a Roland Garros , he also managed to win three Masters Series . He is known lately for being Rafael Nadal's coach since 2016. He was captain of Spain in the 2014 Davis Cup and reached first place in the ATP in 1999, which he held for two weeks.
